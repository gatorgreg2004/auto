Do not leave the scene of the accident until the police have arrived and recorded your statement on what transpired.
If you have not been seriously injured, you should get out of your vehicle and take photographs or a video of the scene of the accident. You should also take the contact information of all drivers involved in the accident as well the contact information of all witnesses who saw how the accident transpired.
