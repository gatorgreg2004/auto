What Should I Do After an Accident? Immediately after an accident, the first thing that you should do is to check if you, your passengers and any other party in the accident has sustained serious injuries. Do not leave the scene of the accident until the police have arrived and recorded your statement on what transpired.
If you have not been seriously injured, you should get out of your vehicle and take photographs or a video of the scene of the accident. The pictures that you take will act as crucial evidence when your lawyer is filing a crash claim. You should also take the contact information of all drivers involved in the accident as well the contact information of all witnesses who saw how the accident transpired.
